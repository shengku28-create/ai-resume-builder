## Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

1. **Use quantifiable achievements** — "Increased sales by 40%" beats "Responsible for sales"
2. **Match keywords exactly** — If the job says "Agile", use "Agile", not "SCRUM"
3. **Keep it concise** — 1 page for <10 years experience, 2 pages max
4. **Proofread with AI** — Catch typos that cost you interviews
