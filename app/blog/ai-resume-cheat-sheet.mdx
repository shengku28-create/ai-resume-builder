# The Ultimate AI Resume Cheat Sheet: 50 Power Words That Get Interviews

*Published: June 09, 2026*

## The 80/20 Rule of Resume Writing

80% of your resume content should consist of **action-oriented power words** that demonstrate impact. 20% should be context.

## 50 Proven Resume Power Words (Categorized)

### Leadership
Orchestrated | Spearheaded | Championed | Directed | Mobilized | Pioneered | Mentored | Authored

### Achievement
Achieved | Attained | Surpassed | Delivered | Generated | Optimized | Transformed | Revolutionized

### Problem-Solving
Resolved | Diagnosed | Investigated | Analyzed | Identified | Streamlined | Simplified | Automated

### Communication
Presented | Negotiated | Persuaded | Influenced | Published | Collaborated | Facilitated | Articulated

### Growth
Expanded | Scaled | Grew | Increased | Accelerated | Multiplied | Drove | Launched

### Efficiency
Reduced | Cut | Saved | Eliminated | Minimized | Streamlined | Consolidated | Accelerated
